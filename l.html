DHANSEVA PLATFORM

Business, Technical & Process Documentation

Project Overview

Dhanseva is a digital lending and loan-management platform that enables users to apply for, receive approval on, and make payments for loans — all within a single integrated ecosystem. Unlike traditional aggregators (like PaisaBazaar), Dhanseva does not depend on any secondary company or bank. All approvals, record-keeping, and payments happen internally on the Dhanseva system through an integrated payment gateway and wallet system.

Key Goals

  • Manage the entire loan lifecycle — application, approval, EMI, and record management — in-house.
  • Integrate payment gateway and wallet systems for fees, EMIs, and agent commissions.
  • Enable data-driven auto-approval logic and eligibility checks.
  • Maintain a transparent, easy-to-use platform for customers, agents, and admins.

User Roles and Responsibilities

Role Description
Customer / User Applies for loans, uploads documents, tracks EMI and payment status.
Agent Assists users in submitting applications, earns commission on approved loans.
Office Staff Verifies documents and forwards valid applications to DSA/Admin.
DSA / Admin Reviews and approves loans, configures products, manages wallets, users, and records.

Loan Application Flow

  1. User selects loan type and uploads required documents.
  2. Platform runs internal eligibility checks based on defined business logic (e.g., salary, credit history, or documents).
  3. Loan is auto-approved if eligibility score meets the threshold; otherwise queued for manual approval by staff/DSA.
  4. On approval, EMI schedule is auto-generated.
  5. Payment gateway collects processing fee or first EMI.
  6. Loan and payment records are stored in user’s dashboard.

Legal & Compliance Highlights

  • KYC (Know Your Customer): Aadhaar, PAN, and income proof collected and verified digitally.
  • Data Privacy: All personal data stored securely with encryption.
  • Consent Mechanism: Users accept T&C before application submission.
  • RBI Norms: Though Dhanseva self-approves, follows digital lending best practices.
